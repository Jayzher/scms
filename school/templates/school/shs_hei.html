SHS-HEI Program

Senior High School to Higher Education Institution Transition Program: Bridging the gap between secondary and tertiary education.

About SHS-HEI Program

The SHS-HEI Program at Holy Child Central Colleges is designed to facilitate a seamless transition from Senior High School to Higher Education. This innovative program bridges the gap between secondary and tertiary education, ensuring students are well-prepared for the academic rigor and expectations of college life.

Program Goals

  • Prepare SHS students for the academic demands of college
  • Develop essential skills for higher education success
  • Provide early exposure to college-level coursework
  • Facilitate informed decisions about college majors
  • Create a supportive transition environment

Program Benefits

  • Advanced standing in college programs
  • Reduced college completion time
  • Potential tuition savings
  • Enhanced college readiness
  • Early access to college resources and facilities
  • Mentorship from college faculty

Program Components

Advanced Placement Courses

College-level courses offered to qualified SHS students that can earn them college credits while still in high school.

  • Mathematics
  • English Composition
  • Introduction to Programming
  • General Psychology
  • Basic Economics

College Mentorship Program

Pairs SHS students with college faculty and upperclassmen who provide guidance, support, and insights into college life.

  • One-on-one mentoring sessions
  • Academic advising
  • Career exploration
  • College application support
  • Study skills development

Campus Integration Activities

Provides SHS students with opportunities to participate in college activities and utilize campus resources.

  • Library access
  • Laboratory use
  • College events participation
  • Student organization involvement
  • Campus tours and orientations

Academic Skills Workshops

Specialized workshops designed to develop the academic skills necessary for college success.

  • Research methods
  • Critical thinking
  • Academic writing
  • Time management
  • Test-taking strategies

Career Exploration Program

Helps students explore potential career paths and align their college major choices with their career goals.

  • Career assessment tools
  • Industry exposure visits
  • Professional guest speakers
  • Job shadowing opportunities
  • Career planning workshops

College Transition Support

Comprehensive support services to help students navigate the transition from high school to college.

  • College application assistance
  • Financial aid guidance
  • Scholarship information
  • Enrollment support
  • First-year adjustment counseling

Eligibility

The SHS-HEI Program is open to qualified Senior High School students who meet the following criteria:

  • Currently enrolled in Grades 11 or 12 at Holy Child Central Colleges or partner schools
  • Maintaining a minimum GPA of 85% or its equivalent
  • Recommendation from SHS teachers or guidance counselors
  • Demonstrated interest in pursuing higher education
  • Parental/guardian consent for program participation

Application Process

  1. Submit Application Form

    Complete the online SHS-HEI Program application form available on the school website.

  2. Provide Academic Records

    Submit your most recent report card and other relevant academic documents.

  3. Teacher Recommendations

    Request recommendations from two current teachers or counselors.

  4. Personal Statement

    Write a 500-word essay explaining your interest in the program and your educational goals.

  5. Interview

    Qualified applicants will be invited for an interview with program coordinators.
