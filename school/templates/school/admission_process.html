Admission Process

Your journey to academic excellence starts here. Learn about our admission requirements and application process.

Welcome to Holy Child Central Colleges

Holy Child Central Colleges is committed to providing quality education to students from diverse backgrounds. Our admission process is designed to be straightforward and supportive, ensuring that prospective students and their families have all the information and assistance they need to make informed decisions about their education.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to admitting students who demonstrate academic potential, character, and a desire to learn. Our holistic admission process considers various aspects of a student's background, achievements, and potential to contribute to our school community.

Why Choose HCCC?

  • Quality education with a strong values formation
  • Experienced and dedicated faculty
  • Modern facilities and resources
  • Comprehensive student support services
  • Vibrant campus life and extracurricular activities

Admission Steps

Step 1: Submit Application

Complete and submit the online application form or visit our Admissions Office to obtain and submit a physical application form.

1

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Recent passport-sized photographs
  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school records/transcripts

What to Expect

  • Academic assessment
  • English proficiency test
  • Mathematics assessment
  • For specific programs: additional subject tests
2

Step 2: Entrance Examination

Take the entrance examination on your scheduled date. The exam helps us assess your academic readiness and place you in the appropriate level.

Step 3: Interview

Participate in an interview with our admissions team. For younger students, parents or guardians are required to join the interview.

3

Interview Focus

  • Educational background and goals
  • Extracurricular interests and talents
  • Character and values alignment
  • Expectations and commitment

What's Included

  • Admission status
  • Program or grade level placement
  • Scholarship information (if applicable)
  • Next steps for enrollment
4

Step 4: Admission Decision

Receive your admission decision. Successful applicants will receive an acceptance letter with enrollment instructions.

Step 5: Enrollment

Complete the enrollment process by submitting required documents and paying the enrollment fees.

5

Enrollment Requirements

  • Completed enrollment forms
  • Medical certificate
  • Original copy of previous school records
  • Payment of tuition and other fees

Admission Requirements

The specific requirements vary depending on the level and program you are applying for. Below are the general requirements for each level.

Basic Education

  • Grade School: Birth certificate, report card from previous school (if transferring)
  • Junior High School: Elementary report card, good moral character certificate
  • Senior High School: Form 138 (report card), Form 137 (transcript), good moral character certificate

College

  • Form 138 (High School Report Card)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • PSA Birth Certificate
  • 2x2 ID pictures
  • For transferees: Transcript of Records, Honorable Dismissal

International Students

  • Passport with valid visa
  • Academic records authenticated by the Philippine Embassy
  • English proficiency test results (if applicable)
  • Alien Certificate of Registration (ACR)
  • Student visa or appropriate visa category

Financial Information

  • Semestral payment
  • Quarterly payment
  • Monthly installment

Holy Child Central Colleges offers various scholarships and financial aid programs to deserving students:

  • Academic Excellence Scholarship

  • Financial Need-Based Scholarship
  • Sibling Discount
  • Alumni Children Discount

For more information about our scholarship programs, please contact the Scholarship Office.

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the application period?

Our regular application period is from January to May each year. However, late applications may be considered depending on slot availability.

Can I apply online?

Yes, we have an online application system. You can visit our website and follow the application instructions.

How long does the admission process take?

The entire process typically takes 2-3 weeks from application submission to admission decision.

Is there an application fee?

Yes, there is a non-refundable application fee of PHP 500 for all levels.

Do you accept transferees?

Yes, we accept transferees subject to meeting our admission requirements and availability of slots.

Ready to Apply?

Our Admissions Office is ready to assist you with your application and answer any questions you may have.
