How to Access E-Library Resources

On-Campus Access

  1. Connect to the campus WiFi network

    All e-library resources are automatically accessible when connected to the HCCC network.

  2. Visit the library computer lab

    Dedicated computers are available for accessing all digital resources.

  3. Ask for assistance

    Librarians are available to help you find and access specific resources.

Remote Access

  1. Log in to the E-Library portal

    Use your student/faculty ID and password to access the portal from anywhere.

  2. Use the VPN service

    For certain resources, you may need to connect via the HCCC VPN. Instructions are available on the IT services page.

  3. Mobile access

    Download the HCCC Library app for convenient access on your mobile devices.

Need help? Contact the library help desk at library@hccc.edu.ph or call +63 938 491 0643 local 123.
