Senior High School Program

Preparing students for college and career through specialized academic tracks and holistic development.

Program Overview

Our Senior High School program is designed to bridge basic education and higher education or employment. We offer specialized academic tracks that prepare students for their chosen career paths while continuing to develop their character and values.

Our Approach

  • Specialized track-based curriculum
  • College and career preparation
  • Industry partnerships and immersion
  • Values integration across all subjects
  • Personalized guidance counseling

Key Features

  • Experienced faculty with industry expertise
  • Modern facilities and laboratories
  • Career exploration and planning
  • College entrance exam preparation
  • Work immersion and research opportunities

Academic Tracks

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)

The STEM track prepares students for college courses and careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

Core Subjects

  • Pre-Calculus
  • Basic Calculus
  • General Biology
  • General Chemistry
  • General Physics

Career Pathways

  • Engineering
  • Medicine and Health Sciences
  • Computer Science
  • Architecture
  • Environmental Science

Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM)

The ABM track prepares students for college courses and careers in business, finance, entrepreneurship, and management.

Core Subjects

  • Business Mathematics
  • Fundamentals of Accountancy
  • Business Finance
  • Principles of Marketing
  • Organization and Management

Career Pathways

  • Accountancy
  • Business Administration
  • Economics
  • Marketing
  • Entrepreneurship

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)

The HUMSS track prepares students for college courses and careers in education, law, social sciences, and communication.

Core Subjects

  • Creative Writing
  • Introduction to World Religions
  • Philippine Politics and Governance
  • Disciplines and Ideas in Social Sciences
  • Introduction to Philosophy

Career Pathways

  • Education
  • Law
  • Psychology
  • Communication Arts
  • Political Science

College Preparation

College Entrance Exam Prep

We provide comprehensive preparation for college entrance examinations, including review sessions, practice tests, and test-taking strategies.

Career Counseling

Our guidance counselors work with students to explore career options, identify strengths and interests, and plan for higher education or employment.

College Application Support

We assist students with college applications, scholarship opportunities, and preparing for interviews and requirements.

Work Immersion Program

Our Work Immersion Program provides students with real-world experience in their chosen fields, helping them develop workplace skills and make informed career decisions.

Program Features

  • Industry Partnerships: We collaborate with local businesses, institutions, and organizations to provide meaningful immersion experiences.
  • Guided Experience: Students are mentored by both school coordinators and workplace supervisors throughout their immersion.
  • Reflection and Assessment: Regular reflection activities and assessments help students process their learning experiences.
  • Portfolio Development: Students create portfolios showcasing their immersion experiences and acquired skills.

Partner Industries

  • Healthcare: Hospitals, clinics, and healthcare facilities
  • Business and Finance: Banks, accounting firms, and local businesses
  • Education: Schools, learning centers, and libraries
  • Information Technology: IT companies and computer service centers
  • Hospitality: Hotels, restaurants, and tourism establishments
