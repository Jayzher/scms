Junior High School Program

Guiding adolescents through a critical phase of development with comprehensive education and character formation.

Program Overview

Our Junior High School program is designed to meet the unique needs of adolescents, providing a balanced approach to academic excellence, personal growth, and social development. We prepare students for the challenges of senior high school while nurturing their individual talents and interests.

Our Approach

  • Learner-centered teaching methodologies
  • Integration of technology across all subjects
  • Emphasis on critical thinking and problem-solving
  • Character development and values formation
  • Preparation for senior high school specializations

Key Features

  • Specialized subject teachers
  • Modern laboratories and learning facilities
  • Comprehensive guidance counseling
  • Diverse extracurricular activities
  • Career exploration opportunities
