Department of Higher Education Institutions

Advancing higher education through quality assurance, innovation, and institutional development.

About DHEI

The Department of Higher Education Institutions (DHEI) at Holy Child Central Colleges is responsible for overseeing and coordinating the development and implementation of policies, programs, and projects related to higher education. We work to ensure that our college programs meet the highest standards of academic excellence and remain relevant to the needs of our students and society.

Our Mission

To foster a culture of excellence in higher education by promoting quality assurance, innovation, and continuous improvement in academic programs, research, and community engagement.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in providing accessible, relevant, and quality higher education that produces competent, ethical, and socially responsible graduates who contribute to national development.

Key Functions

Quality Assurance

We develop and implement quality assurance mechanisms to ensure that our higher education programs meet or exceed national standards.

  • Program accreditation
  • Faculty development
  • Curriculum review and enhancement
  • Learning outcomes assessment
  • Institutional quality audits

Academic Development

We lead initiatives to enhance academic programs and teaching methodologies to provide students with the best possible education.

  • Curriculum development
  • Instructional innovation
  • Academic policies formulation
  • Educational technology integration
  • Learning resource development

Research and Innovation

We promote a culture of research and innovation among faculty and students to contribute to knowledge creation and societal development.

  • Research agenda development
  • Faculty and student research support
  • Research publication and dissemination
  • Industry-academe collaboration
  • Innovation and entrepreneurship programs

Programs and Initiatives

The DHEI implements various programs and initiatives to enhance the quality of higher education and support the development of students, faculty, and the institution as a whole.

Faculty Development Program

A comprehensive program designed to enhance the professional competencies of faculty members in teaching, research, and extension services.

  • Teaching methodology workshops
  • Research capability building
  • Graduate studies scholarship
  • Professional certification support
  • International faculty exchange

Student Development Program

Programs and activities designed to enhance students' academic performance, leadership skills, and personal growth.

  • Academic excellence awards
  • Student leadership training
  • Research mentoring program
  • Career development workshops
  • Student exchange programs

Curriculum Development Initiative

A systematic approach to reviewing, updating, and enhancing academic curricula to ensure relevance, quality, and alignment with industry needs.

  • Outcomes-based curriculum design
  • Industry-aligned program development
  • Curriculum review and enhancement
  • Interdisciplinary program integration
  • International curriculum benchmarking

Quality Assurance System

A comprehensive system for monitoring, evaluating, and improving the quality of academic programs and institutional operations.

  • Program accreditation support
  • Institutional quality audits
  • Learning outcomes assessment
  • Quality management system development
  • Continuous improvement processes
