About Our Seal

The official seal of Holy Child Central Colleges represents our institution's identity, values, and aspirations. Designed with careful consideration of our heritage and mission, the seal serves as a visual embodiment of what we stand for as an educational institution.

The seal is used on official documents, diplomas, certificates, and other formal communications of the institution. It symbolizes the authority and credibility of Holy Child Central Colleges and is a source of pride for our entire academic community.

Each element of the seal has been thoughtfully incorporated to represent different aspects of our educational philosophy and institutional values, as detailed below.

Elements of the Seal

The Circle

The circular shape of the seal represents wholeness, unity, and the continuous cycle of learning and growth. It symbolizes our commitment to providing a well-rounded education that encompasses all aspects of human development.

The Open Book

At the center of our seal is an open book, representing knowledge, learning, and academic excellence. The open pages symbolize our commitment to education and the pursuit of truth through study and research.

The Torch

The torch in our seal represents enlightenment, guidance, and the illuminating power of education. It symbolizes our role in lighting the path for students as they journey through life, providing them with the knowledge and values that will guide them.

The Stars

The stars in our seal represent excellence, aspiration, and the limitless potential of our students. They symbolize our commitment to helping students reach for the stars and achieve their highest potential in all their endeavors.

The Globe

The globe element in our seal represents our global perspective and commitment to preparing students for success in an increasingly interconnected world. It symbolizes our vision of producing graduates who are globally competitive and culturally sensitive.

The Shield

The shield in our seal represents protection, strength, and honor. It symbolizes our commitment to upholding the highest standards of integrity and our role in safeguarding the values and traditions that define our institution.

Colors of the Seal

Blue

Represents knowledge, integrity, and stability

Gold

Symbolizes excellence, achievement, and wisdom

White

Represents purity, clarity, and truth

Green

Symbolizes growth, hope, and renewal

Our Motto

"He's Leader Tomorrow, Walk with Us Today"

Our motto encapsulates the essence of our educational mission at Holy Child Central Colleges. It reflects our commitment to developing future leaders through quality education and holistic formation.

The phrase "He's Leader Tomorrow" emphasizes our focus on leadership development and preparing students for future roles of responsibility. "Walk with Us Today" invites students to join our educational journey, highlighting the collaborative nature of the learning process and our supportive community.

Usage Guidelines

The school seal is an important symbol of our institution and should be used with respect and in accordance with the following guidelines:

Authorized Use

  • Official school documents and publications
  • Diplomas, certificates, and awards
  • School website and official social media accounts
  • Official school merchandise and promotional materials

Unauthorized Use

  • Alteration of the seal's design, colors, or proportions
  • Use in a way that implies endorsement of products or services not affiliated with the school
  • Use in contexts that could damage the reputation of the institution
  • Commercial use without explicit permission from the administration

For inquiries regarding the use of the school seal, please contact the Office of the President or the Public Relations Office.
